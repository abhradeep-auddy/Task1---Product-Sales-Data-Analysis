Product Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard (Advanced)
---> Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail dataset to evaluate product-level performance, regional sales distribution, customer contribution, and profitability trends.

The focus is on identifying revenue drivers and profit patterns to support better pricing, product strategy, and regional decision-making.

---> Tools Used
Excel → Data cleaning and preprocessing
SQL (PostgreSQL) → Data aggregation and transformation
Power BI → Interactive dashboard and visual analytics

---> Key Insights
North region generates the highest total revenue, followed closely by East and West
Sales distribution across products is fairly balanced, with slight dominance by high-demand items (e.g., Tablet, Laptop)
Monthly profit (~39.15K) remains relatively stable, but does not scale proportionally with sales
Total revenue (~130.50K) shows a gradual declining trend over time, indicating possible demand slowdown
A small segment of customers contributes significantly to total revenue (high-value customers)

---> Business Recommendations
Focus on top-performing regions (North, East) for expansion and targeted marketing
Identify and promote high-revenue products while reviewing low-performing ones
Improve profitability strategies, as profit is not increasing proportionally with revenue
Implement customer retention programs for high-value customers
Investigate causes of declining sales trend and introduce corrective strategies (seasonal offers, product bundling, etc.)
📂 Project Files

 ---> Conclusion

This project highlights how data can be used to analyze multi-dimensional business performance, including products, regions, customers, and time trends.
It demonstrates the ability to convert raw data into meaningful insights and actionable recommendations, supporting effective business decision-making.
